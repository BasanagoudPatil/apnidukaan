ApniDukaan — Project Overview



1\. Introduction



ApniDukaan is a digital platform that helps small vendors establish their own independent online stores. The platform aims to simplify the process of creating an online presence for businesses that traditionally operate offline or through informal channels.



The project focuses on enabling vendors to represent their businesses digitally while maintaining their individual brand identity.



---



2\. Problem Statement



Many small vendors and local shop owners lack the technical resources or knowledge required to create and manage an online store. As a result, they rely heavily on physical stores or social media platforms to reach customers.



These approaches often limit their visibility, scalability, and ability to manage their business efficiently in the digital environment.



---



3\. Purpose of the Project



The purpose of ApniDukaan is to create a platform that allows vendors to easily establish their own digital storefront. The system aims to reduce the complexity of building an online store while enabling vendors to manage their business presence independently.



---



4\. Project Scope



The scope of the project includes designing and developing a platform where vendors can create and manage their own online store. The platform will provide a structured environment for vendors to represent their businesses digitally and interact with customers through an online interface.



The project focuses primarily on supporting small vendors and local businesses.



---



5\. Target Users



The platform is intended for the following users:



• Small business owners

• Local shop vendors

• Independent sellers

• Customers who want to browse and purchase from local stores



---



6\. Project Vision



The vision of ApniDukaan is to empower local businesses by enabling them to build their own digital storefront and participate in the growing online marketplace.



---



7\. Project Status



The project is currently in the planning and documentation phase. System design, architecture planning, and requirement analysis are being completed before development begins.
