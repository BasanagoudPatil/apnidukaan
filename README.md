\# ApniDukaan



\## Overview

ApniDukaan is a digital platform designed to help small vendors establish their own independent online stores. 

The project focuses on enabling local businesses to create a digital presence and reach customers online.



\## Vision

The vision of ApniDukaan is to empower small businesses by providing a simple and accessible way to build 

and manage their own online storefront.



\## Target Users

\- Small business owners

\- Local shop vendors

\- Independent sellers



\## Project Status

🚧 Project is currently in the planning and development phase.



\## Future Scope

The project aims to support the digital growth of local businesses and provide scalable solutions 

for independent online stores.



\## License

This project is currently under development.
